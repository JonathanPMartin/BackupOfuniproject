Deputy principal private secretary post added to 10 other job adverts to fill vacancies in deputy prime minister’s office that staff were ‘too scared to enter’ 
Dominic Raab’s office is scrambling to fill yet another vacancy, as formal complaints against the deputy prime minister’s conduct continue to mount.
An internal civil service job advert seen by
 shows the deputy prime minister’s office is looking for another senior mandarin on as much as £76,000 to start in the new year.
The role, for deputy principal private secretary, will involve working closely with Mr Raab.
