Kim Jong-un says country has achieved ‘remarkable’ power in fields of ‘politics, military, economy and culture’ 
Kim Jong-un at a plenary meeting of the Workers’ Party of Korea in Pyongyang
 has hailed his nation’s growing power, which he claimed has increased “remarkably” in the fields of “politics, military, economy and culture”.
During a meeting of the Workers’ Party of Korea, Mr Kim highlighted “successes and progress” in 2022 while urgiing officials to launch “more exciting and confident policies.”
’s military this year test-launched a record number of missiles, many of them nuclear-capable weapons capable of reaching the US mainland and its allies
