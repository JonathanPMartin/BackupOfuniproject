Hitting the right note! Princess Elisabeth of Belgium wows in a figure-hugging blue dress as she joins her siblings and parents at traditional Christmas concert in Brussels
Belgian Royal Family attended a Christmas concert at Royal Palace in Brussels
Princess Elisabeth, 21, looked stylish in a blue gown for the festive occasion
She was accompanied by King Philippe, 62, Queen Mathilde, 49, and her siblings
 19:51, 20 December 2022 
 20:28, 20 December 2022 
 hit all the right notes in a captivating blue gown tonight as she and her family attended their annual 
 concert at the Royal Palace in Brussels.
The traditional event saw the 21-year-old join her brother Prince Emmanuel, 17, her sister Princess Eléonore, 14 and her parents, King Philippe, 62, and Queen Mathilde, 49, as well as her aunt and uncle, Princess Claire and Prince Laurent.
For the festive occasion, the whole family dressed in all their finery, with Elisabeth stealing the show in a chic form-fitting floor-length dress with a daring split.
The Princess is currently studying 'History &amp; Politics' at 
's Lincoln College but has travelled home for the holidays.

The young royal donned minimal makeup, with just a dash of sparkly eyeshadow, eyeliner and blush, topped with a rosy glossy lip.
